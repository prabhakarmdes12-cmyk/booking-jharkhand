The Queen of Chotanagpur

Perched at an altitude of 1,128 meters, Netarhat is the highest point of the Chotanagpur plateau. Once a summer retreat for British governors, it remains an untouched paradise of pear orchards, pine forests, and dramatic sunsets.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far is Netarhat from Ranchi? expand_more
Netarhat is approximately 154 kilometers from Ranchi. The drive takes about 4 hours through scenic winding roads and lush green forests.
Is it safe to drive to Netarhat at night? expand_more
We recommend completing your journey before sunset. The road has several hairpin bends and passes through forest areas which are better navigated in daylight for safety and scenery.
What should I pack for Netarhat? expand_more
Even in summer, evenings can be cool. Pack light woolens for the nights. During winter (Oct-Feb), heavy woolens are necessary as temperatures can drop significantly.
